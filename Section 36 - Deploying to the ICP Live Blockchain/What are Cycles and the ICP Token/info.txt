ICP Token (Internet Computer Token) - They're tradable tokens, one way to acquire them is by buying,
but another way is through the Network Nervous System (NNS), which basically controls the ICP. To
get tokens through it, it has a voting system that gives away tokens.
A third way is Dfinity developer grant program. The last way is acting like a data center.

Cycles - Tokens can be exchanged for computational cycles in your canisters. While there's cycles
the program works.
