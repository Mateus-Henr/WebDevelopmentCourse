Cookies
They are used to save information about a session on a website.
Notice that they are also used for advertisement. Once you have cookies of a product
for example, other websites can throw some ads about that same item.
Cookies are created as a result of a POST request.

Session is a type of cookie which is a period of time that your browser interacts
with the server. Log in is an example where cookies get saved in order to not ask
again.
